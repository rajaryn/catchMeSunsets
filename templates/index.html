catchMeSunsets

It started with a simple conversation.

We realized how easy it is to miss a sunset while caught up in the blur of everyday life. We wanted a way to freeze that golden hour—and more importantly, we needed a place to go to watch the sun go down when we inevitably missed it ourselves.

That is how catchMeSunsets was born.

There are no profiles here. No followers, no egos, no noise. It is completely anonymous by design. Every pin on this map is just a quiet moment where someone, somewhere, stopped to look up at the sky and decided to share it with you.

Drop a pin. Share your sky. Catch the sunsets you missed.
