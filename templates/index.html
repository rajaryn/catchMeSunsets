About Vesper

vesper (n.) — the evening star; twilight.

It started with a simple conversation — about fleeting sunsets, a beautiful moon, all the small moments where you stop everything to look up at the sky. The ones that are so easy to miss, and yet somehow feel the most important.

We wanted a place to hold onto that pause. Somewhere to catch the skies we missed, shared quietly by strangers who stopped, just like us. That's how Vesper was born.

No profiles, no followers, no noise — just a map of anonymous moments. Somewhere out there, someone looked up and decided to share it with you. Drop a pin. Share your sky.
