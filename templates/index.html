About Vesper

vesper (n.) — the evening star; twilight.

It started with a simple realization. Between the endless demands of my computer science degree and the daily blur of organizing college placements, I realized how incredibly easy it is to miss a fleeting sunset or a quiet, brilliant moon.

I wanted a way to freeze that twilight—and more importantly, I needed a place to go to watch the sky change when I inevitably missed it myself. That is how Vesper was born.

There are no profiles here. No followers, no egos, no noise. It is completely anonymous by design. Every pin on this map is just a quiet moment where someone, somewhere, stopped to look up at the sky and decided to share it with you.

Drop a pin. Share your sky. Catch the views you missed.
